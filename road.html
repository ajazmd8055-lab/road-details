About Road Safety

Road safety refers to the measures taken to prevent accidents and injuries on roads. Traffic awareness helps people understand traffic rules and follow them properly.

Important Traffic Rules

  • Always wear a helmet.
  • Wear seat belts.
  • Follow traffic signals.
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Do not drink and drive.

Road Safety Tips

  • Drive at safe speed.
  • Use zebra crossing.
  • Maintain safe distance.
  • Avoid distractions.
  • Follow lane discipline.

Traffic Awareness

Traffic awareness programs educate people about road safety and help reduce accidents.
